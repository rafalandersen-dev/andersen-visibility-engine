# Milo Growth — Private Beta launch kit

Status: **Private Beta RC2** · Production: https://milogrowth.com

A reusable kit for inviting and running the first small cohort of testers. Milo Growth
is an AI growth *planner* for small businesses (SEO opportunities, site audit, competitor
gaps, authority planning, AI-search readiness, and editable content drafts). It is a
planner, not a publisher — nothing is auto-published.

## 3. Tester instructions (one full loop, ~10–15 min)

1. Sign up at https://milogrowth.com (email + password, or Google/Apple).
2. **Create a project** — your business name, website, type, location, languages, and a short
   description. The richer the context, the sharper the output.
3. **Add 2–3 services or products** you actually sell.
4. **Run Site Audit** — review findings; try turning one into an opportunity.
5. **Generate SEO Opportunities** — your first batch of visibility ideas.
6. **Run one of** Competitors / Authority / AI Visibility — review the analysis and convert a
   gap or action into an opportunity (single + "create from top" bulk).
7. **Create one content asset** in the Editor (pick any opportunity → "Create content" → choose
   an asset type) and edit it.
8. Refresh the page once — confirm everything you created is still there.

Follow the on-screen **"Next recommended step"** banner on the Dashboard; it walks you through
this same path.

## 4. Feedback questions (6–8, Google Form / Typeform)

1. **Setup:** How easy was creating your first project and getting your first plan? (1–5)
2. **Value:** Which module felt most useful — Site Audit / Competitors / Authority / AI
   Visibility / Opportunities / Calendar / Editor? (pick one + why)
3. **Output quality:** Did the AI-generated opportunities and content feel specific to *your*
   business, or generic? (1–5 + an example)
4. **Trust:** Did anything feel inaccurate, overpromised, or like it claimed something it didn't
   actually do? (open)
5. **Confusion:** Where did you get stuck, or have to guess what to do next? (open)
6. **Missing:** What's the one thing that would make this a "yes, I'd pay for it"? (open)
7. **Pricing read:** Starter 149 PLN / Growth 249 PLN per month — too low / about right / too
   high? Would you pay it?
8. **NPS:** How likely are you to recommend Milo Growth to another business owner? (0–10)

Send the link in the post-test email — don't gate first use behind it.

## 5. Known caveats

**Share with testers (sets honest expectations):**

- Milo is a **planning tool** — it does **not** auto-publish, post, or send anything.
- **AI Visibility is readiness planning, not live AI rank tracking** — it does not query
  ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, or Google AI Overviews.
- Site Audit & Competitor analysis read **only the homepage** (best-effort). A failed fetch
  falls back to your project details with a visible note.
- AI output is a **first draft** — review before using. By design it won't invent fake stats,
  prices, or ranking guarantees.
- Generation takes ~10–30s per run and depends on the AI provider; if it's busy you'll get a
  clear retry message rather than a silent failure.

**Internal / not yet live (don't promote):**

- **Billing is disabled** ("Coming soon"). All beta testers currently have full access,
  including project creation and exports; paid-tier limits are not enforced yet.
- Each project stores **one analysis per module** (re-running replaces the previous result).
- Pricing is displayed in **PLN**.
- **Single workspace per user**; no team seats.
- Non-owner accounts are capped at **5 projects**.

## 6. 3–5 tester launch plan

**Goal:** validate setup clarity, output quality, and trust framing with 3–5 real
small-business owners over ~1 week.

- **Pick 3–5 testers** across your real ICP — a mix of service vs product businesses, and at
  least one Swedish-market and one Polish/English business (Milo supports all three; test
  multilingual output).
- **Day 0 — invite:** send the email (section 1). Stagger 1–2 per day so issues surface before
  the next wave.
- **Day 0 — you on standby:** after each signs up, check Lovable Cloud logs / their workspace
  for errors and be ready to DM help if anyone stalls at Setup.
- **Each tester completes one full loop** (section 3): create project → add services → run
  audit → generate opportunities → run one analysis module → create one content asset.
- **Day 2–4 — collect:** send the questions (section 4) ~24h after they've explored, while it's
  fresh.
- **Day 5–7 — synthesize:** group feedback into (a) blocking bugs, (b) confusing copy/UX,
  (c) output-quality gaps, (d) pricing signal. Fix only **blocking bugs** during beta; log the
  rest for post-beta.
- **Guardrails:** keep the cohort small until you've watched AI-gateway spend (top-up balance)
  under real concurrent use; billing stays "Coming soon"; don't widen past 5 until any blocking
  bugs are cleared.

---

## 7. Success criteria (decide "ready for wider beta")

- ≥ **4 of 5** testers complete the full loop unaided.
- Output rated **"specific to my business" ≥ 4/5** by a majority.
- **Zero** trust/accuracy red flags (no one feels Milo overclaimed or invented data).
- **NPS median ≥ 7.**

If all four hold, widen the cohort. If not, fix the top blocker(s) and re-test with a fresh
small group before expanding.
